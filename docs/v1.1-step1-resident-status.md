# v1.1 STEP 1 — Resident Status Safety Layer

## What was added

- Introduced resident `status` field
- Added `dischargedAt`, `lastSeenCensusAt`, and `dischargeBatchId`
- Established soft-discharge model (no deletion of residents)

## Workflow change (Plain Language)

Before:
- Residents missing from census could be lost or overwritten

Now:
- Residents are NEVER deleted
- If removed from census → they become **discharged**
- Their appointment history remains fully accessible

## Why this matters

- Prevents data loss
- Supports regulatory reporting
- Allows historical and future appointment tracking
- Enables reopening discharged residents
