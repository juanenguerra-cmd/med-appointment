# v1.1 STEP 2 — Smart Census Engine

## What this introduces

This step adds a **Smart Census Reconciliation Engine** that replaces the old overwrite behavior.

## New behavior

Instead of replacing residents:

- Existing residents are matched using MRN or name+room
- Matching residents are updated and marked ACTIVE
- Missing residents are NOT deleted
- Missing residents are marked DISCHARGED
- Discharged residents remain accessible in the system

## Key capabilities

- Soft discharge (no data loss)
- Reactivation of returning residents
- Full audit-ready summary of census changes
- Support for historical appointment tracking

## Summary Output Example

- Total incoming residents
- Created residents
- Updated residents
- Reactivated residents
- Discharged residents
- Unchanged residents

## Why this matters

- Prevents accidental deletion of residents
- Preserves appointment history
- Enables reporting on discharged residents
- Supports regulatory and audit workflows
